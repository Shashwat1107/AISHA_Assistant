Here is a sample sick leave letter:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, ZIP]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Title]
[Company Name]
[Company Address]
[City, State, ZIP]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to request a sick leave for [number] days, starting from [date] and ending on [date]. Unfortunately, I have fallen ill and am currently unable to come to work.

My symptoms include [list specific symptoms, such as fever, headache, etc.]. I am under the care of a doctor and am following their instructions to recover. I expect to make a full recovery within [number] days and will return to work as soon as I am able.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will do my best to catch up on any missed work as soon as I return. If there is anything I can do in the meantime to mitigate the impact of my absence, please let me know.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Please note that you should customize this letter to fit your specific situation and company policies. You may also want to include any additional information, such as:

* A specific doctor's note or medical certificate
* A plan for completing any urgent or critical tasks while you are out
* A request for accommodations or modifications to your work schedule upon your return
* Any other relevant details that may be helpful to your employer

Remember to keep a professional tone and be honest about your situation.
